Logo design portfolio

This is a selection of logo design projects, logos, symbols, icons, letter marks, monograms, logotypes, logomarks, corporate patterns, created between 2007 – 2021.

Many of the shown logos were gallerized on Logopond, featured in the Graphic Design and Branding galleries on Behance, included in the LogoLounge, Logopond, Los Logos and Logonest books series.

In 2018 the LogoLounge Annual Trends Report article featured my Mind Heroes M letter mark while my CoinBase logo exploration was included in the 2020 LogoLounge Logo Trends Report.⁠
